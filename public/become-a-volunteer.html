Who are our volunteers?

Experts in their craft

Our volunteers know what they're doing. They create learning opportunities based on skilled knowledge that go beyond the curriculum, so students can understand the real world.

From diverse backgrounds

Students are the future of our workforce, and therefore need experiences in all areas. Our volunteers come from all areas of expertise to engage a variety of student interest.

Committed to the job

Working with kids can sometimes be a challenge, but our volunteers know how worth it it is. They're excited to roll up their sleeves and do their part in educating our students.
